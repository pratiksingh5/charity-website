Indulgence Meets
Sustainability

Nestled in the Konkan region, The Pebble Stay is a new-age
experiential home-stay that offers a serene view of the Arabian sea.

“Pre-book your stay or take home authentic Konkan produce, and support the cause of sustainable vacations”.

Bespoke Experiences


At The Pebble Stay opt for a sustainable lifestyle and reduce your carbon footprint. Moreover, you can experience life and enjoy the authentic Konkan lifestyle.


Comfortable Interiors

A serene picturesque bedroom with an attached bathroom with premium accessories.

Private Outdoor Space

Soak in the majestic sea view and sight dolphins from the room while sipping a hot cup of coffee.

Tastings Of Konkan

Relish the freshest possible seasonal food and indulge in an authentic Konkan food experience.

Pebble Stay Partner Program

The Pebble Stay Partner Program is a crowdfunding effort to build the next phase of a sustainable premium experience. With a mission to create a positive impact and lower our carbon footprint, we urge you to pre-book your stay and contribute towards realising the sustainable vacation vision.
